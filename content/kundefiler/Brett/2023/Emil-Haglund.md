## Signalkjede
- Trådlås mottaker -> AB-switch, svart
- AB-switch, gul -> EP Booster
- EP Booster -> Tuner
- Tuner -> AB-switch, rød
	- AB-switch, grønn -> DI, grønn
- AB-switch blå -> Duke
- Duke -> Blackstone
- Blackstone -> Boss EQ
- Boss EQ -> Carbon Copy
- Carbon copy -> Flint
- Flint -> Iridium
- Iridium -> DI, blå
